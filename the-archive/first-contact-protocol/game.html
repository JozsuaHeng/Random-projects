◈ PROTOCOL BRIEFING ◈

This is a gamebook: read the transcript on the left, then select a course of action. Each choice advances the log to a new entry.

FIELD CACHE: specimens and equipment you recover are stored here. Some courses of action require specific items. Select an item twice to consume it (USE) or wear it (equip).

VITALS: if HEALTH, OXYGEN, or SANITY reaches zero, your transmission ends. The array does not wait for stragglers.

TEST YOUR LUCK: some readings are uncertain. Roll two dice — seven or under reads favorably. A resonant shard on your person widens that margin to eight.

Your log autosaves. RESET UPLINK erases it and begins again.
