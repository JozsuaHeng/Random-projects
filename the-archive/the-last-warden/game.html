⚜ THE CODE OF ARMS ⚜

Every page of this chronicle ends in a choosing. Read the account on the left, then take up whichever path you will — the page turns to wherever your choice leads.

SATCHEL & GEAR: what you find, you carry. Some paths only open to those bearing the right relic or blade. Strike an item twice to consume it, or don wearable gear onto your person.

HEALTH, STAMINA, SANITY: let any fall to nothing and your account ends there. The keep shows no mercy for the unprepared.

TEST YOUR LUCK: some moments are decided by the fall of two dice. Seven or under, fortune favors you. A Warden's Token or Boneward Charm on your person widens that favor to eight.

Your progress is remembered between visits. To swear anew is to forsake it and begin again at the gatehouse.
