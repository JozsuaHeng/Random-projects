HOW TO PLAY

This is a gamebook — like the paperbacks where every choice tells you which page to turn to. Read the page on the left, then pick a choice to turn to that page.

ITEMS you find go into your backpack (bottom right). Some choices only unlock if you're carrying the right item. Click items marked USE to consume them for a boost.

STATS: if HEALTH, OXYGEN, or SANITY hits zero, your story ends. Watch the bars.

TEST YOUR LUCK: some moments roll two dice. Roll 7 or less to succeed. Carrying the Luck Charm lets you succeed on 8.

Progress autosaves. NEW GAME wipes it and starts over.
